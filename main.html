Symbol Number - Game Rules

How to Play

  1. Setup: Deal 6 cards to each player. Place the rest in the bank.
  2. Start: Place the top card of the bank in the middle. The first player matches the card's symbol.
  3. Turns: Play a card matching either the symbol or the number of the card on the table. (first turn is symbol then the second is number then symbol again in rotations)
  4. Draw: If you can't play, draw a card from the bank. If the bank is empty, match either symbol or number.
  5. Winning: The first player to use all their cards wins!

Special Rules

  • If the bank is empty and you can't play, you may match either symbol or number.
  • If no players can make a move, the game ends in a draw.
